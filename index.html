About Me

My name is Neamen Dobamo and I am a full-stack Web Application Developer and Software Developer, currently living in Colorado, CO. I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Metro state university, and my primary focus and inspiration for my studies is Web Development. In my free time, I study human computer interface and the psychology of human computer interaction. I am both driven and self-motivated, and I am constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques. I am very passionate about Web Development, and strive to better myself as a developer, and the development community as a whole.
